# OctoAcme — Project Initiation Guide

**Document Owner:** Project Manager  
**Contributors:** Product Manager, UX Designer, Technical Writer, QA Lead, DevOps Engineer

## Purpose
Define the initial steps to validate and authorize work, align stakeholders, and create a lightweight plan.

## When to use
Whenever a new project idea or feature proposal is ready to be explored.

## Goals
- Confirm business need and measurable outcome
- Identify stakeholders & champions
- Define success criteria and initial timeline
- Decide go/no-go for planning

## Minimum Deliverables
- Project One-pager (Problem, Goal, Success Metrics)
- Stakeholder list & communication plan
- High-level timeline and key milestones
- Initial risk list
- Resource needs (team roles, rough effort estimate)

## Kickoff Meeting Agenda Template
The kickoff meeting should include all relevant roles. Use the following agenda:

1. **Project overview** — PM presents the one-pager (15 min)
2. **Roles & responsibilities** — Confirm assignments for PM, PdM, UX Designer, QA Lead, DevOps Engineer, Technical Writer, and Developers (10 min)
3. **Success metrics & acceptance criteria** — PdM leads; UX Designer shares usability goals (10 min)
4. **Risks & dependencies** — Team identifies top risks; DevOps Engineer surfaces infrastructure or deployment risks (10 min)
5. **Communication cadence** — PM confirms standup schedule, stakeholder update frequency, and escalation paths (5 min)
6. **Q&A and next steps** (10 min)

## Initiation Checklist
- [ ] One-pager completed and reviewed by Product Lead
- [ ] Sponsor / Stakeholder alignment (email or meeting)
- [ ] Kickoff meeting held with all required roles (PM, PdM, Developer, UX Designer, QA Lead, DevOps Engineer, Technical Writer)
- [ ] Decision: Approve to move into planning?
- [ ] Create repo or project board skeleton
- [ ] Add initial artifacts to repo (docs/ or .copilot/)
- [ ] Document owner and contributors assigned for all project documents
- [ ] Initial communication plan drafted (owner: PM)

## Quality Gate — Initiation Sign-off
Move to planning **only** when all of the following are true:
- Success metrics are clear and agreed upon by PdM and Stakeholders
- Stakeholders agree on priority and business case
- Team availability is confirmed and all roles are assigned
- Initial risk register created with at least one owner per risk
- Document owner assigned for the project one-pager and communication plan
